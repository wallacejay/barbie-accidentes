Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when requesting assistance after a car accident or workplace accident, registering during community events, completing online forms, or contacting us for information about our services. This information may include your name, phone number, and any details related to your request for assistance.

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Respond to requests for assistance related to car accidents or workplace accidents
  • Provide referrals to legal professionals or service providers
  • Send updates regarding your request or case status
  • Provide follow-up support or requested information
  • Send information about services, pricing, or available promotions when relevant to your request

Communication Methods

By providing your phone number or contact information, you consent to be contacted regarding your request for assistance or services. Communication may occur through SMS text messages, phone calls, or messaging platforms such as WhatsApp when appropriate for responding to your request or providing assistance.

SMS Communications

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your request for assistance, service updates, referrals, follow-up communication, or relevant service information.

Message frequency may vary depending on your request or case status. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. You may also request to stop receiving messages by contacting us directly through our website, sending us a message, or by calling our phone number.

Once your opt-out request is received, we will process your request and stop sending SMS messages to your number. If you need assistance, you may contact us directly through our website or phone number (623) 628-7619.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may only be shared with legal professionals or service providers when necessary to assist with your request.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is used, please contact us through our website or by phone.
